Chapter 1 — Introduction

This sample chapter introduces the purpose and scope of the General & Subsidiary Rules (G&SR) adapted for Konkan Railway. Use this fragment as a template for real chapters.

Scope

The rules in this book are intended to provide practical guidance to running staff, loco pilots, guards and station staff for safe train operations on Konkan Railway.

How to use

Use the menu (table of contents) to jump to other chapters. The app caches each chapter on first open so it becomes available offline.
