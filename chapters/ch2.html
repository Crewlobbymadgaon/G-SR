Chapter 2 — Definitions & Interpretations

This sample chapter contains common definitions used throughout the G&SR. Replace or expand entries with official definitions from your source documents.

Key terms

  • Train: A set of vehicles moved along a track under a single movement authority.
  • Block section: The portion of track between two block instruments or signals.
  • Loco pilot: The authorized driver of a locomotive.

Interpretation note

Where the text refers to specific posts (station master, guard, loco pilot), it is assumed to mean the person holding that post or an authorized deputy.
