CHAPTER II

RULES APPLYING TO RAILWAY SERVANTS GENERALLY

S.R.2.01(1)
Copies supplied may be hard copy or electronic copy or relevant extracts thereof.
2.02
UPKEEP OF THE COPY OF RULES
  1. (a)
    keep his copy readily available when on duty,
  2. (b)
    keep it posted with all corrections,
  3. (c)
    produce the same on demand by superiors,
  4. (d)
    obtain a new copy if lost or defaced, and
  5. (e)
    ensure staff under him comply with this rule.
2.03
KNOWLEDGE OF RULES
  1. (a)
    be conversant with rules relating to his duties,
  2. (b)
    pass prescribed examinations,
  3. (c)
    ensure staff under him comply with clauses (a) and (b),
  4. (d)
    explain rules to staff where necessary.
S.R.2.03(1)
Train crews and Guards of adjoining Railways must be conversant with Subsidiary Rules and local instructions of this Railway.
S.R.2.03(2)
Railway servants prescribed spectacles must possess two pairs while on duty.
2.04
ASSISTANCE IN OBSERVANCE OF RULES

Every railway servant shall render assistance and report promptly any breach of rules.

2.05
PREVENTION OF TRESPASS, DAMAGE OR LOSS
  1. (1)
    responsible for security of railway property under his charge.
  2. (2)
    endeavour to prevent trespass, theft, injury and fire.
2.06
OBEDIENCE TO RULES AND ORDERS

Every railway servant shall obey all rules and lawful orders.

2.07
ATTENDANCE FOR DUTY

Every railway servant shall attend duty as fixed by the Railway Administration.

2.08
ABSENCE FROM DUTY
  1. (1)
    No railway servant shall absent himself without permission.
  2. (2)
    Illness shall be reported immediately.
S.R.2.08
Unauthorized absence renders the servant liable to disciplinary action.
2.09
TAKING ALCOHOL OR DRUGS

No railway servant shall be under influence of alcohol or drugs while on duty or within eight hours before duty.

2.10
CONDUCT OF RAILWAY SERVANTS
  1. (a)
    be neat and tidy,
  2. (b)
    be prompt and courteous,
  3. (c)
    not solicit illegal gratification,
  4. (d)
    assist the public correctly,
  5. (e)
    give name and designation when asked.
